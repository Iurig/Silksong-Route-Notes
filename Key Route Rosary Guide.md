
### Key Route Rosaries Text Guide

This guide uses rosary numbers assuming no rosaries are lost, totaling 506 rosaries. You can make sure you haven't lost too many rosaries by either counting if you've lost more than 6 total, or using the rosary check near the end.

##### Moss Mother

---

30 - grab rosary string as you cross the pit in Moss Grotto

##### Silk Spear

---

60 - 2 rocks behind breakable wall to the right of Beastfly Pogo

Hit shortcut lever before silk spear room

##### Swift Step

---

30 - Pickup silk spear string

17 - Hanging strings when going down silkspear room

31 - Kill 3 guys

Go up ant pogo objects to drop shortcut outside bell room

7 - Kill Smelt Shoveler at deep docks entrance (nail + spear)

7 - Kill smelt Shoveler after flintstone flyer pogo

##### Sprint

---

7 - Kill Smelt Shoveler after sprint

7 - Kill Smelt Shoveler before climbing up marrow

10 - Grab hanging rosaries before chest shortcut room

45 - Secret rocks

30 - Bell room

76 - Chest room

23 - Big skull guy + rock (3 nail + spear)

5 - Pilgrim pouncer in the same room

40 - Shakra room rock

---

At this point you want to have AT LEAST 419 rosaries, ideally, 422 or more.

After collecting the Shakra room rock there are 35 rosaries

to collect from pilgrims or the hanging strings,

so grab however much you need.

---

23 - Kill 3 enemies in lever room below silkspear

28 - Jump across to below wormways entrance to break the rock

Fall down on the far right of the pit to be able to reach the elevator

30 - Break rosary strings above the elevator

DONT FORGET THE ELEVATOR LEVER
